I was born in Tomahawk, Wisconsin on April 24th, but
when I was seven my family moved from Wisconsin to New Mexico. There I attending Mosaic Academy, a public charter school, and eventually attended Aztec High School, where I graduated with Honors.
My parents split up in early high school, which brought on a lot of challenge in my family. I became an angry and spiteful teenager who reclused herself in her room and hated the world.

During this period of time, I didn't know who I wanted to be, or what career I wanted to go into. It was then I was made aware of a college course I could take that was
about the introduction to web development and graphic design.
I fell in love with it immediately, and wanted to
pursue web development as a whole. Hence why I moved here!
My mom and my little brother still live in New Mexico, while
my father and I live up here.
My goal for the future is to
move to Denver, Colorado not only to be closer to my family
and friends, but to also have access to
a lot more job opportunities. I hope to be able to
work for a large company there, but also have the ability
to do freelance designing as well.
